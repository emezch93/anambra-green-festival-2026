🌍 SWEEP Foundation NG

Building a Circular Economy
in Anambra Since 2020

SWEEP Foundation NG has been active in Anambra State since 2020, working closely with the Ministry of Environment, local government authorities, and community groups to build practical solutions for waste management and the circular economy.

The Foundation's state presence is anchored through the Anambra Waste Recyclers Association (AWRA), which it helped establish in 2021 — positioning Anambra as a recognized hub for grassroots waste recovery and green enterprise development in Southeast Nigeria.

Our approach remains partnership-driven, focused on delivering measurable economic opportunities while reducing environmental pollution across the state through field engagement in Awka, Onitsha, Nnewi, and other LGAs.

About the Festival

Anambra's Flagship Green Event

The Anambra Green Enterprise and Circular Economy Festival is Anambra State's flagship event for World Environment Day 2026. It brings together traders, artisans, green entrepreneurs, manufacturers, investors, and policymakers to showcase practical solutions for waste-to-wealth, renewable energy, and sustainable production.

Scheduled for 5th June 2026 at the Prof. Dora Akunyili Women Development Centre, Awka, the Festival features exhibitions, business linkages, and policy dialogues designed to turn green ideas into viable enterprises — giving Anambra's MSMEs direct access to markets, finance, and partnerships.

Programs & Activities

What's Happening at the Festival

Four high-impact tracks designed for real business outcomes

🏪 01 — Exhibition

Exhibition & Marketplace

Over 100 MSMEs will exhibit recycled crafts, organic fertilizers, solar products, eco-packaging, and upcycled goods to buyers and investors.

🤝 02 — Linkages

Business Linkage Sessions

Structured meetings between manufacturers, waste aggregators, and traders to negotiate supply agreements for recyclables and green products.

💡 03 — Knowledge

Knowledge Exchange

Short, practical sessions on financing for green MSMEs, compliance, product design, and market access in the circular economy.

🏛️ 04 — Policy

Policy Dialogue

A high-level session with the Ministry of Environment, LGAs, and private sector leaders on policies that support green enterprise growth in Anambra.
